About Us

It all started with grandpa...

Grandpa had a dream. He wanted to make sure his small town had full access to delicious pork. He created his business with the goal of sharing and caring for everyone near him. Through word of mouth, and passing strangers, his business grew famous outside the town. He became a legend in the nearby areas. He became a household name. From the farm, to your table, grandpa wants to make sure you get the best pork around.

Our Values

Throughout our history, we have evolved into becoming a shop that only supports the most humane of farms. We do not support any farms that disregard the importance of humane farming. We do not support overcrowding or any abuse. Our pigs come from farms that offer plenty of space, food, and shelter. Since we are a family owned business, we know that family matters.
